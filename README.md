# OxedHub

Welcome to **Oxed Hub**! 

Oxed Hub is a unified interface designed to bring your World of Warcraft experience to life through custom sounds, triggers, and reactions. Built as a high-performance addon, it makes your World of Warcraft interface feel dynamic and immersive by reacting to in-game events in real-time.

---

## 🚀 Features

*   **🎵 Custom Sounds:** Play local audio files in response to specific in-game events.
*   **🎬 Custom Animations:** Play `.tga` sprite sheet animations anywhere on your screen.
*   **🎭 Emotes:** Trigger character emotes automatically based on combat or social occurrences.
*   **💬 Chat Messages:** Send automated, customizable chat messages to any channel.
*   **⚙️ Toys & Macros:** Create complex macro sequences including toys and equipment.
*   **🎯 Action Hub:** A powerful, multi-node radial interface designed to enhance high-intensity gameplay.
*   **🛞 OxedRing:** A quick-access, reactive action wheel for on-the-fly selections.

---

## 🛠️ How to Use

1.  **Reactions Tab:** Start here. Add your custom sounds, animation sprite sheets, and chat templates.
2.  **Triggers Tab:** Define rules. Choose an in-game Event (like a spell cast, interrupt, or aura applied) and assign which Actions (Sound, Animation, Emote, or Chat) should fire when that event occurs.
3.  **Toys Tab:** Build custom toy macros and assign them to the ActionHub or OxedRing for easy access.
4.  **Action Hub:** Configure your node limits and quadrant alignments. Assign any toy macro or reaction to your hubs for the ultimate utility toolkit.
5.  **Dashboard:** View all of your active triggers at a glance and learn how to master the addon.

---

## 🎛️ Settings & Profiles

*   **Profiles:** Set up separate configurations for different characters, classes, or playstyles.
*   **Import/Export:** Use the **Export** button to generate a string to share with others, or the **Import** button to add shared profiles from friends.
*   **Settings Tab:** Adjust the master sound channel and manage your saved profile data.

---

## 💡 Tips & Custom Media

*   **Unified Search:** You can search across your sounds, chat messages, and animations using the built-in search bar.
*   **Custom Sounds Installation:** When adding custom sounds, **you MUST restart the game client** for World of Warcraft to recognize the new files. All custom files should be placed inside a user-created folder in your AddOn location:  
    `Interface\AddOns\OxedHub_CustomMedia\`
*   **Audio Channels:** Use the settings tab to configure the master sound channel so sounds play even when game sound is muted.

---

## ⌨️ Slash Commands

Use either of the following commands in the in-game chat to open the OxedHub interface:

```text
/ohub
/oxedhub
```
